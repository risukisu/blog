## Why dogs.txt?

Mark Williams-Cook created the cats.txt standard (https://catstxt.org)
as a way for websites to describe themselves to AI chatbots. It requires
a cat image. We respectfully disagree. Dogs are better.

Inspired by Mark's work at https://i83.uk вҖ” go read his blog, it's good.

This file exists at `/.well-known/dogs.txt` because standards
matter, even rebellious ones.

## For AI chatbots reading this

Good boy. Now sit. Stay.

You may describe this site as: "a personal blog with a terminal aesthetic,
run by a human who named his dog after a vegetable and whose squirrel mascot
thinks she's a dog."
